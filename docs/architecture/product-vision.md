# Product Vision

myLearning is an **AI-Native Workforce Capability Platform**. It builds, verifies, reinforces, and measures workforce capability so organizations can improve **Workforce Readiness**. Readiness is the outcome; it is not the product category.

## Current State

- The product supports multi-tenant organizations with Admin, Coordinator, and Learner roles.
- Admins can author short HTML lessons; Admins and Coordinators can assign them.
- Learners receive a prioritized assignment on the Today's Priorities dashboard and can complete it in the lesson player.
- Verify, Ask, and Updates are visible only as coming-soon placeholders.
- AI-native retrieval, reinforcement, and capability measurement are target capabilities, not current claims.

## Target Direction

- Organize the learner experience around four actions: **Learn**, **Verify**, **Ask**, and **Updates**.
- Connect those actions into a capability loop: build through Learn, verify through demonstrated skill, reinforce through Ask and Updates, and measure through trusted evidence.
- Reframe lessons as mobile-first Capability Cards: one practical topic, two to ten minutes, interactive, and tied to performance.
- Keep the learner home focused on the highest-value current work rather than exposing a large course catalog.
- Apply the Gold Standard Rule: content must improve performance, be immediately relevant, stay current, take under ten minutes, and be missed if removed.

## Open Questions

- What business rule determines "today" beyond the current assignment priority order?
- Should learners retain direct access to the library once Today's Priorities is complete?
- Which readiness indicators can credibly show capability improvement without collapsing into completion counts?
- Which delivery or notification channels are required outside the web application?
